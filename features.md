Here are several features you can add to your todo app to attract and retain users:

1. **Reminders & Notifications**: Allow users to set due dates and receive browser or email notifications for upcoming or overdue tasks.
2. **Recurring Todos**: Support for daily, weekly, or custom recurring tasks.
3. **Subtasks/Checklists**: Let users break down a todo into smaller actionable items.
4. **Search & Filter**: Enable searching, filtering, and sorting by priority, completion, or due date.
5. **Progress Tracking**: Show completion stats, streaks, or productivity charts.
6. **User Accounts & Sync**: Allow users to sign up and sync their todos across devices (using email, Google, or social login).
7. **Collaboration**: Share lists or tasks with others for team or family use.
8. **Attachments**: Allow users to add notes, images, or files to todos.
9. **Customizable Themes**: Offer more color themes and personalization options.
10. **Voice Input**: Add support for adding todos via voice commands.
11. **Offline Support**: Let users access and edit their todos even without an internet connection.
12. **Gamification**: Add badges, achievements, or rewards for completing tasks.

Implementing any of these features can make your app more engaging and useful for a wider audience.
